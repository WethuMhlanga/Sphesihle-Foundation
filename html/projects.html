Our Projects

Educational Sessions

We conduct educational sessions focusing on various life skills essential for young women. These sessions cover topics such as health, hygiene, financial literacy, and personal development.

Purpose

To provide young girls with the knowledge and skills they need to lead healthy, independent lives.

Scheduling

Sessions are held every Saturday from 10 AM to 1 PM.

Process

Each session is led by experienced mentors and includes interactive activities, discussions, and hands-on learning experiences.

Entrepreneurship Program

Our entrepreneurship program teaches young girls how to farm crops and manage small businesses. They learn the entire process from planting to selling, gaining valuable business and leadership skills along the way.

Purpose

To empower girls to become financially independent by teaching them practical business skills.

Scheduling

The program runs for six months, with weekly sessions every Wednesday from 2 PM to 5 PM.

Process

Participants are guided through the steps of starting and running a business, including market research, financial planning, and product sales.

Leadership and Business Skills

We provide workshops and mentoring to help young women develop leadership and business skills. These sessions are designed to inspire and equip them with the knowledge needed to succeed in their personal and professional lives.

Purpose

To cultivate leadership qualities and business acumen in young girls, preparing them to become influential leaders in their communities.

Scheduling

Workshops are held monthly on the first Saturday of each month from 9 AM to 12 PM.

Process

Our workshops include guest speakers, group activities, and one-on-one mentoring sessions to ensure a comprehensive learning experience.
