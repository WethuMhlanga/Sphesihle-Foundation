Welcome to Sphesihle Foundations

Empowering young girls from underprivileged backgrounds through education and entrepreneurship.

Learn More

Our Mission

To provide support and offer knowledge to young girls from underprivileged backgrounds, empowering them to become leaders and entrepreneurs.

Our Vision

To create a world where every young girl has equal opportunities to thrive and succeed no matter their background.

The WHYs

Empowerment

We believe in empowering young girls with the skills and knowledge they need to transform their lives and communities.

Equality

We strive for gender equality and believe in providing equal opportunities for all young girls, regardless of their background.

Community Impact

Our goal is to make a positive impact in the communities we serve, fostering long-term sustainable development.

Leadership Development

We aim to nurture leadership qualities in young girls, preparing them to become future leaders and change-makers.
